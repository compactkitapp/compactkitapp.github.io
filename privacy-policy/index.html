Privacy Policy

Your information, explained plainly.

Effective date: April 8, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how CompactKit may collect, use, and protect information when you use the app, visit this website, or contact support.

2. Information you provide

If you contact CompactKit by email, feedback form, or another support channel, you may provide information such as your name, email address, message contents, screenshots, or attachments you choose to send.

3. App and device information

CompactKit may receive limited technical information needed to operate, maintain, and improve the service, such as app version, device type, operating system version, language, and basic diagnostic details.

4. Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, and Google Ads

CompactKit uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand app usage, monitor stability, and diagnose failures. Depending on how the app is used and configured, these services may process information such as app-instance identifiers, session and usage statistics, approximate geolocation, device and app information, stack traces, relevant app state, and device metadata associated with crashes or errors. CompactKit uses this information only to monitor performance, investigate bugs and crashes, and improve app quality. It is used only for app analytics, diagnostics, and stability improvement.

CompactKit may also use Google Ads services to display and measure ads in the app. Depending on configuration, these services may process data such as device and app information, identifiers, approximate location, ad-interaction data, and performance information to support ad delivery, fraud prevention, and measurement. Where required, ad-related processing is subject to applicable user consent and platform privacy settings.

5. File handling

Files selected for compression are processed only to provide the requested app functionality. CompactKit is designed so that file compression and related operations happen on your device, and your files are not uploaded to third-party servers as part of normal use of these features. Users should only process files they have the right to use.

6. How information may be used

Information may be used to provide app functionality, respond to support requests, troubleshoot bugs, improve performance, prevent abuse, and meet legal obligations.

7. Sharing

CompactKit does not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers that help operate the app or website, or when required by law, contract, or a valid legal request.

8. Your choices

You may choose not to submit optional information. You can also contact CompactKit to request updates or deletion of support communications where legally permitted.

9. Children's privacy

CompactKit is not intended for children under the age required by local law to consent to data processing without parental involvement.

10. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date above will be revised when changes are published.

11. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to compactkitapp@gmail.com.
